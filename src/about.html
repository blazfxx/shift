Why I built Shift

Honestly? I got tired of shady file converter sites with ad walls, fake download buttons, random limits, and weird account requirements for basic stuff.

I just wanted to convert files quickly and move on. So I built Shift to do exactly that: free, local conversion with no signup and no watermark nonsense.

Everything runs in your browser with FFmpeg WebAssembly, which means your files stay on your device. Nothing gets uploaded to my server.

I run BOXU, and this is one of those tools I wanted to exist in the world, so I shipped it.

If you want to check how it works, the code is public on @blazfxx.
