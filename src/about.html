About Shift

Built because file converters got ridiculous.

I wanted a converter that feels honest: drop a file, choose a format, done. No popups, no fake buttons, no weird bait-and-switch.

Honestly? I got tired of shady converter websites with ad walls, fake downloads, newsletter traps, and random limits for basic tasks.

So I built Shift to be simple and direct. Everything runs in your browser with FFmpeg WASM, so your files stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded to my server.

I run BOXU, and this is one of those tools I wanted to exist, so I shipped it.

Private by default

All conversion happens locally in your browser. Your files do not leave your computer.

Actually free

No account required, no watermarks, no paywall for common formats.

Open and inspectable

Source is public on @blazfxx.
